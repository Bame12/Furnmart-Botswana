Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery options?

We offer free delivery within Gaborone and Palapye. For other areas in Botswana, delivery fees vary based on distance. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days.

Do you offer assembly services?

Yes! Professional assembly is available for P 150 per item. Our team will assemble your furniture and remove all packaging materials.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all furniture. Items must be in original condition with all packaging intact. Return shipping is free for defective items.

How does AR visualization work?

AR (Augmented Reality) lets you see furniture in your actual space using your phone's camera. Simply tap the "AR View" button on any product and follow the on-screen instructions.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we partner with several financial institutions to offer flexible payment plans. Contact us for more details on financing options.

Can I customize furniture?

We offer customization options for select items including fabric choices, colors, and dimensions. Visit our showroom or contact us to discuss your requirements.
