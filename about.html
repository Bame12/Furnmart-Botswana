About Furnmart Botswana

Transforming houses into homes since 1998

Our Story

Founded in 1998, Furnmart Botswana began with a simple mission: to provide quality, affordable furniture to families across Botswana. What started as a small showroom in Gaborone has grown into the nation's leading furniture retailer.

Over the past 27 years, we've furnished over 50,000 homes and built lasting relationships with our customers. Our commitment to quality, affordability, and exceptional service has remained unchanged.

Today, we're proud to combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology, including AR visualization, to help you make confident furniture decisions for your home.
